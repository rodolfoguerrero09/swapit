Safe Exchange Guidelines

1. Verify User Profiles

Always check the profile of the person you're exchanging with. Look for verified users with good ratings.

2. Public Meeting Points

Meet in public places like coffee shops or technology stores during daylight hours. Never exchange at private residences.

3. Inspect Before Exchanging

Always thoroughly test and inspect the technological item before completing the exchange. Verify functionality and condition.

4. Use Platform Protection

All exchanges are recorded and protected by SWAP IT. We mediate disputes and ensure fair transactions.
